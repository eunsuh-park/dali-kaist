About the Lab

We conduct research on publics and their communicative behaviors
to promote a healthy information ecosystem for a sustainable future.
We tackle pressing information challenges of our time,
including pseudo-information, public biases, and failing information markets.

Research Focus

Understanding how and why publics engage in communicative actions, examining proactiveness and reactiveness in information acquisition, selection, and transmission.

Mission

Developing theoretical frameworks and practical solutions to address cognitive biases, epistemic inertia, and information market failures in our digital age.

Impact

Contributing to a more informed and resilient society through evidence-based research and strategic communication interventions.
