About the Lab

The DaLI (Debiasing and Lay Informatics) Lab tackles pressing information challenges of our time, including pseudo-information, public biases, and failing information markets. We conduct research on publics and their communicative behaviors to promote a healthy information ecosystem for a sustainable future.
